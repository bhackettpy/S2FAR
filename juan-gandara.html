Juan Gandara

Undergraduate Student

About

Juan Gandara is an undergraduate studying Mechanical Engineering at Oklahoma State University. He earned an Associate of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Tulsa Community College before transferring to OSU. Prior to that, he completed the Advanced Pre-Engineering program at Tulsa Tech. Juan has worked as a Mechanical Engineering intern at Mingo Aerospace in Owasso, contributed to engineering projects, participated in the OSU Space Cowboys Rocket Team Club, and took part in FIRST Robotics Competitions during high school. Juan joined the Simulation to Flight Applied Research (S2FAR).
